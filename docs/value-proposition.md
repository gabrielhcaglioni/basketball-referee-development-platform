# Value Proposition

## Problem

Referees receive valuable feedback after games, but most of this information is lost over time.

## Current Situation

Development is based mainly on memory and isolated conversations.

There is little historical tracking of growth.

## Proposed Value

The platform helps referees understand their development journey.

## What the referee receives

- Personal development history
- Structured feedback
- Identification of recurring patterns
- Comparison between self-perception and external evaluation
- Long-term evolution tracking

## Success Definition

A referee should be able to answer:

- What am I improving?
- What are my recurring challenges?
- What patterns appear in my games?
- What should I focus on next?
