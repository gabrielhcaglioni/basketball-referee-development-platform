# Product Definition

## Product Name

Basketball Referee Development Platform

## Purpose

Support the continuous development of basketball referees through structured feedback and self-assessment.

## Main Users

### Referee

- Complete self-assessments
- Review personal history
- Track development

### Coordinator

- Register observations
- Provide feedback
- Track referee progress

### Federation (Future)

- Analyze referee development
- Identify training needs
- Monitor officiating programs

## Core Principle

The platform does not focus on punishment.

The platform focuses on development.

## Main Question

What patterns emerge over time in the referee's development?
